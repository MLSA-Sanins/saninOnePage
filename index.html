How It Started?

Nothing comes without hardwork and dedication,so do us,the SANINS. Loads of efforts made it happen. From a group of three to a community of techno-aspirants.

  • We started as a community of three in the year 2019. Stayed together to learn and grow.
  • We took in the similar minded people to keep focus on learning and filling the gaps.
  • It was in the year of 2021 January that two of our mates got selected for the Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors program. That is when we decided to expand.

What we are?

From novice to experienced the community comprises of all minds. Our group of learners joins you with a global network that is passionate about technologies.

  • The code of conduct is to maintain a workplace ambience where generosity is nothing much to ask.
  • This community is powered by a few Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors who encourage you to make a difference. Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors program * amplifies your skills and prepares you for real world problems.

Community Architecture

  • With an assurance of total support and better experience the community is further categorised into three categories:-

    • SUDO SANINS:
      The small fraction of core members.
    • SANINS:
      The company here represents the metabolism of the group.
    • JUNO SANINS:
      The newbies and budding developers.

    • Journey

    • Being able to lead to help others to grow has always been a source of inspiration. And doing it via community can't get any better.
    • That's where the Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors Arshad Khan and Ankan Biswas impacted the objective.
    • Both have different directions regarding technology, yet both are quick learners. From looking various ways to explain applications of tech-world to starting community, they believed in growth, no matter how small things start.
    • And justifying their acquired positions and skills, here they are to fill in the gaps of knowledge.
