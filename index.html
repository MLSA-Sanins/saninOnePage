How It Started?

Nothing comes without hardwork and dedication,so do us,the SANINS. Loads of efforts made it happen.From a group of three to a community of techno-aspirants.

  • We started as a community of three in the year 2019.Stayed together to learn and grow.
  • We took in the similar minded people to keep focus on learning and filling the gaps.
  • It was in the year of 2021 Jan that me along with one of my mate got selected for the Microsoft Student Learn Ambassador Program. We decided to expand.

What we are?

From novice to experienced the community comprises of all minds. More than anything we are learners with eagrness to help and grow.

  • The code of conduct is to maintain aworkplace ambience where generosity is nothing much to ask.
  • The Sanins further consists of three categories.
    • SUDO SANINS : The small faction of core members.
    • SANINS: The company here represents the metabolism of the group.
    • JUNO SANINS: The newbies and budding developers
